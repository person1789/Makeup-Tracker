How to use Classroom Tracker

Students Tab

1. Add Students

Go to the Students tab to build your roster. Enter names one by one, or import them via CSV. All attendance tracking relies on having your students set up first.

2. Create Assignments the Fast Way

In the Calendar view: Double-click any day to assign work on that day. Then, Single-click a future day to set the due date. The assignment menu will immediately open, pre-filled with your selected dates!

3. Take Attendance

Go to the Attendance tab, select today's date, and click the blank button next to a student to cycle their attendance to P (Present) or A (Absent).

4. Manage Makeup Work

The system automatically tracks missing work! If a student is marked Absent during an assignment's active window, the Makeup Work tab will calculate a new extended "Makeup Due Date" based on how many days they missed. Once they submit it, click "Mark Complete" to clear it.
